A note on these numbers This calculator is a planning aid, not financial advice or a guaranteed forecast. Suggested tiers (Community / Standard / Premium) are based on general NYC wellness-event pricing patterns; your actual market will vary by neighborhood, modality, instructor brand, time of year, and venue partnership. The "Test a price" and "Hit a profit goal" tools project outcomes based on the inputs you provide and assumptions about attendance — they don't predict the future. We recommend treating the calculator output as a starting point, then validating against (a) what comparable creators in your specific niche are charging, (b) actual quotes from your venue and vendors, and (c) realistic attendance based on your own marketing reach. Auric Movement LLC isn't liable for outcomes that differ from the calculator's estimates.
